What’s a SEP IRA?
A Simplified Employee Pension (SEP) plan allows business owners to contribute toward their employees’ retirement account as well as their own. Contributions are made to an Individual Retirement Account and can be invested in cryptocurrency through our platform. Click here to learn more about a SEP IRA.
